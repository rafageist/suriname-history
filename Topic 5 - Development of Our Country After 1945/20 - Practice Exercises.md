### Student Textbook Content

1. In groups, you will search in Lesson 1 for one of the following key concepts:
   - Development Aid
   - Prosperity Fund
   - Operation Grasshopper
   - Wageningen Plan

   The information you find, you write down, and the groups take turns telling the rest of the class about their key concept.

2. Make a word web in your notebook. Write the word "Brokopondo Plan" in a circle. Around it, write five words or concepts that, according to you, are related to it. Also tell why for each word or concept.

3. With development aid, various projects were carried out in our country. Below are a number of projects and their descriptions. Find which project each description refers to.

   You can choose from the following projects:
   - Wageningen Plan
   - Brokopondo Plan
   - Housing project
   - Victoria oil palm project
   - Operation Grasshopper
   - Operation Gwamba

   Descriptions:
   a. During this project, various airstrips were built in our interior.
   b. Due to urbanization and the housing shortage, there was a need for houses. Therefore, various of these projects were carried out.
   c. In the west of our country, during this project, a rice business and rice polders were established.
   d. When the water in the reservoir began to rise, this project was carried out to save the animals in the flooded area.
   e. The planting of oil palms during this project was meant to ensure the production of palm oil.

4. In groups, you will prepare a plan. This plan can be about, for example, the expansion of the school, but also about the construction of a sports field or the building of houses, a daycare center, or a media library.

   When writing the plan, you take the following questions with you:
   - What is the goal?
   - What needs to be done?
   - By whom will it be done?
   - How much money will it cost?
   - How will you get the money?
   - How long will it take?
