# Practice Exercises - Topic 2

---

### Practice Exercises

1. In groups, look at the images.
   Discuss with each other on which pictures children are learning something and what they are learning.

2. In groups, find the following years in the lesson and briefly write down why the year is mentioned.
   - 1760
   - 1809
   - 1844
   - 1863 (2x)
   - 1876

3. Write the correct names of the people next to the descriptions below.

   - In the same year that slavery was abolished, this person wrote the first history book of our country.
   - In Wanica, a school is named after this person, who opened a primary school in our country in 1809.
   - Between 1878 and 1910, this person kept strict supervision over education in our country and the number of children going to school increased.

4. In groups, discuss the following questions about education in our country:
   a. How was education for enslaved children different from education for white children?
   b. Why do you think education for Indigenous children improved after slavery was abolished?
   c. What challenges do you think students in our country still face today regarding education?
