# Topic 3: Different Cultures in Our Country

## Practice Exercises: Topic 3 Review

**1.** In Lesson 1, examples of cultures from our country can be seen in images 3 through 7. In groups, choose one of the cultures and write or draw examples of that culture. These can be examples that can be seen in images in this lesson. Besides that, also think of examples yourselves. The different groups present their work to the class.

**2.** In your notebook, make a drawing or write a poem about experiencing culture. Do it in your own way. Choose what has to do with culture for you. Also tell why you chose it. A few students can present their work to the class.

**3.** In groups, choose a national holiday in our country that has to do with one of our cultures. Give answers to the following questions:
- a. Which culture does this national holiday belong to?
- b. What is being celebrated or commemorated?
- c. On or around what date is this day?
- d. In the group, also write what you yourselves have done on this day.

**4.** Puzzle. Fill in horizontally:

1. You must not look down on other cultures, but you must ... them.
2. In the past, the own culture of our people was ...
3. At this place, the Maroons changed their panji for other clothing.
4. Which word can you use for the mixing of different cultures?
5. In our country, many different cultures live together. We call that a ... cultural society.
6. Who wrote the poem Wan bon?
7. In what language did Johanna Schouten-Elsenhout write her poems?
