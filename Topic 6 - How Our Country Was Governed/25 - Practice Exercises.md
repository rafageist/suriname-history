# How Our Country Was Governed

## Processing Assignments

---

### Student Textbook Content

1. In class, you make a timeline from 1650 to 1975. This timeline can be drawn on the board or hung on the wall. The following years come on the timeline:
   - 1683
   - 1795
   - 1799 – 1816
   - 1866
   - 1937
   - 1948
   - 1975

   Tell which changes in the government of our country took place in those years.

2. In groups, you make a drawing about the government of our country. Image 3 can be used as an example. But it may also be done differently. Indicate in the drawing for which period it is. Also draw the people. Indicate which part is allowed to vote and according to which suffrage. Also draw the government of the country, by whom it is formed, and how they are elected or appointed.

3. In groups, you choose one of the following years. You tell about the suffrage in that year and who was allowed to vote according to that suffrage.
   - 1866
   - 1937
   - 1948

4. In groups, you choose one of the following forms of government. Write how the rulers were elected or appointed.
   - Governor
   - Political Council
   - Colonial States
   - States of Suriname (from 1937 and from 1948)
