INTRODUCTION

At school, the flag is raised every day. This beautiful flag has been our national flag since 1975. In 1975, our country became independent. That is what this theme is about. In the first lesson, it tells about independence and the national symbols that our country then received. In the second lesson, you learn that in 1980, a coup was carried out in our country. In 1986, a civil war also broke out. About that war and the peace that was signed, is told in the third lesson.
