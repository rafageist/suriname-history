# Practice Exercises - Topic 3

---

### Practice Exercises

1. In Lesson 1, examples of cultures from our country can be seen in images 3 through 7. In groups, choose one of the cultures and write or draw examples of that culture. These can be examples seen in images in this lesson. Also think of your own examples.
   The different groups present their work to the class.

2. In your notebook, make a drawing or write a poem about experiencing culture. Do it in your own way. Choose what for you is related to culture. Also explain why you chose it. Some students can present their work to the class.

3. In groups, choose a national holiday in our country that is related to one of our cultures. Answer the following questions:
   a. Which culture does this national holiday belong to?
   b. What is being celebrated or commemorated?
   c. On or around which date is this day?
   d. In the group, also write down what you yourself did on this day.

4. Puzzle. Fill in horizontally:
   1. You should not look down on other cultures, but you should _ them.
   2. In the past, the own culture of our people was _.
   3. At this place, the Maroons exchanged their panji for other clothing.
   4. Which word can you use for the blending of different cultures?
   5. In our country, many different cultures live together. We call that a _ cultural society.
   6. Who wrote the poem Wan bon?
   7. In which language did Johanna Schouten-Elsenhout write her poems?
