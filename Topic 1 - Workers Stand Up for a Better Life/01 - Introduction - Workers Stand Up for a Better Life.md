# Topic 1: Workers Stand Up for a Better Life

## Introduction: Workers Stand Up for a Better Life

When the conditions under which people work and live are not good, they protest against them. In this topic, you will learn that many workers in our country became unemployed at the beginning of the 20th century. There was no work, and therefore people could not earn money. People were poor and protested against the poor conditions. In lesson one, it is explained that conditions were also bad in other countries. There was an economic world crisis. In lesson two, you will hear about Louis Doedel and Anton de Kom, two Surinamese people who stood up for the interests of workers. In lesson three, an explanation is given about the origin and purpose of trade unions.
